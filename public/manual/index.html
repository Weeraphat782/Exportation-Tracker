Customer Portal User Manual

Step-by-step guide for signing in at cargo.omgexp.com, creating a quote request, selecting your product type, uploading export documents, and tracking approval through to shipment.

Overview

The OMG Experience Customer Portal is your export workflow hub for time-sensitive, temperature-controlled, and compliance-critical air freight. After signing in, you can submit a Request Quote, tell us what you are shipping, provide pallet and destination details, attach export documents, and monitor progress while our team reviews and approves your request.

The end-to-end workflow looks like this:

Sign in (Google) → Request Quote → Choose commodity → Enter details → Submit request → Upload documents → Pending approval → Quoted & shipped

Before you begin

  • A registered customer account linked to your Google email address.
  • Access to the Google account you used when registering (for Continue with Google sign-in).
  • Basic shipment information: destination, pallet dimensions, and weight.
  • Export documents ready to upload (PDF, JPG, PNG, or Excel). Maximum file size: 10 MB per file.
  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).
First-time users: If you do not yet have an account, click Register on the login page or visit cargo.omgexp.com/site/register to create one with Google or email before following this guide.

1. Sign in with Google

  1. Open the login page
    In your browser, go to https://cargo.omgexp.com/site/login.
  2. Click Continue with Google
    On the sign-in card you will see the heading Welcome back and the subtitle Sign in to your customer portal. Click the white button labelled Continue with Google (with the Google “G” icon).
  3. Complete Google authentication
    You will be redirected to Google's sign-in page. Enter your Google email address (for example, your.name@gmail.com) and click Next. Enter your Google account password on the next screen and click Next again.
  4. Grant access (if prompted)
    If Google asks to share your name, email, and profile with the OMG Experience portal (via Supabase authentication), click Allow or Continue.
  5. Land on the Customer Portal
    After a brief Signing in… loading screen, you are redirected to /portal. First-time users may see a Set up your company screen before reaching the My Shipments dashboard (see Section 1b).
Security note: Never share your Google password. OMG Experience staff will never ask for it. If you see Login for Admin / Staff, that link is for internal staff only — customer users should always use the Continue with Google button on this page.

Alternative: Sign in with email

Below the Google button, you can also sign in using the OR EMAIL section by entering your registered email and password, then clicking Sign In →. Google sign-in is recommended if your account was created with Google.

1b. Company setup (first login only)

If this is your first time signing in, you will be taken to Set up your company at /portal/setup before you can use the portal. This information appears on your quotations and shipping documents.

  1. Review pre-filled fields — your full name and contact email are populated from your account.
  2. Enter Company Name — your registered business name (required).
  3. Enter Address — your full company address (required).
  4. Optionally fill Tax ID / VAT number and phone number.
  5. Click Continue to Portal → to save and open the My Shipments dashboard.

2. Portal dashboard

After login, you arrive at My Shipments — your home dashboard at https://cargo.omgexp.com/portal.

Use the search bar to filter by quotation number or destination. Click the green + Request Quote button (top-right) at any time to create a new request.

3. Start a new quote request

To create a new export request, either click Request Quote in the left sidebar or the green + Request Quote button on the dashboard. Both open the Request a Quote wizard at /portal/quotations/new.

1. Commodity 2. Details 3. Documents

The wizard has three steps shown as a progress bar at the top: Commodity → Details → Documents. Complete each step in order. You can go back using the ← Back button on later steps.

3a. Step 1 — Commodity (choose your product)

The first screen asks “What are you shipping?” with the instruction: Select the type of product you plan to export. This helps us prepare the right document checklist.

Click one of the four product cards:

ProductDescriptionNotes
Cannabis Medical-grade cannabis flowers, extracts, biomass Includes GACP certification options and full TK-form document checklist.
Hemp Industrial hemp & CBD products (Hemp Letter included) Requires Hemp Letter among export documents.
Kratom Kratom products for export Includes permits, TK forms, and shipping documents.
General General export shipments Simplified document checklist for non-specialised cargo.
  1. Review the four product options and click the card that matches your shipment.
  2. The selected card will highlight with a coloured border and checkmark.
  3. Click the green Next → button at the bottom to proceed to Step 2.
Your product choice determines which documents appear in Step 3. Choose carefully — if unsure, select the closest match or email cargo@omgexp.com before submitting.

3b. Step 2 — Details (shipment information)

Step 2 is titled Enter shipment details. Fill in all required fields marked with a red asterisk (*).

Shipping destination Required

In the field labelled Shipping Destination (Where to?) *, enter where the cargo is going. Example placeholder: e.g. Bangkok to Tokyo / Warehouse Address. This field is mandatory — you cannot submit without it.

Pallet dimensions

The blue How it works info box explains: Enter your pallet dimensions. After submit, you can attach export documents right away — or skip and upload later from your shipment page.

For each pallet, provide:

FieldDescription
LengthPallet length (cm).
WidthPallet width (cm).
HeightPallet height (cm).
Weight (kg)Total weight of the pallet in kilograms.
QuantityNumber of identical pallets (defaults to 1).
  • At least one pallet is required.
  • Click + Add Pallet to add more pallets for multi-pallet shipments.
  • Click the remove (×) button on a pallet row if you have more than one and need to delete it.

Additional notes Optional

Use the Additional Notes text area for special requirements, preferred shipping dates, or any other instructions. Placeholder text: Any special requirements, preferred shipping dates, or other notes…

Optional service checkboxes

Depending on your product type, you may also see:

  • Has Data Logger (attach MSDS) — tick if your shipment includes a temperature data logger. Upload the MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheet) in Step 3.
  • Need Phytosanitary Certificate service? — tick if you want OMG Experience to arrange the plant health certificate required by many destinations. Additional fees may apply and will be included in your quote.
  • Using GACP from another farm / Check if purchased from another farm — tick if your GACP certificate comes from a different farm (Cannabis/Hemp products).
  • Thai GACP or GACP Certificate — upload your certification document in Step 3 when applicable.
If any required field is empty, the form shows Please fill in all required fields. and highlights missing items. Complete everything before submitting.

Submit the request

When all required fields are complete, submit from the bottom of Step 2:

  1. Review your destination, pallet dimensions, and notes.
  2. Click the green Submit Quote Request button (send icon).
  3. The button shows Submitting… while your request is saved.
  4. On success, a toast confirms Quote request submitted! and the wizard advances to Step 3.
After submission, your request enters the Pending Approval queue. Our logistics team reviews your commodity type, destination, pallet data, and any uploaded documents before issuing a formal quote.

What if submission fails?

  • Please fill in all required fields. — complete missing pallet or destination data.
  • Shipping Destination is required — enter a destination before submitting.
  • At least one pallet is required — add pallet dimensions.
  • A quotation with these details already exists — check My Shipments for a duplicate.
  • Failed to submit request. — check your internet connection and retry.

3c. Step 3 — Documents (upload export files)

After submitting Step 2, you see a confirmation: Quote received! with the message Want to attach your export documents now? Step 3 is labelled Attach documents (optional).

The page shows a Document checklist for [your product] organised into sections. Each document type has a Choose file… upload area. You can upload multiple files per document type.

Accepted file formats and limits

  • Accepted formats: .pdf, .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .xlsx, .xls
  • Maximum size: 10 MB per file
  • Files over 10 MB are rejected with an error message showing the file name.

How to upload

  1. Scroll through each document section in the checklist.
  2. Click Choose file… (or the upload area) next to each required document type.
  3. Select one or more files from your computer. Selected files appear listed under each type.
  4. Repeat for all documents you have ready.
  5. When files are selected, click Upload [N] file(s) & continue to upload them all. A success toast confirms: Uploaded N file(s).
  6. Alternatively, click Skip for now if you are not ready to upload. You can upload documents later from your shipment detail page.
  7. Click Done — view my shipment to go to the shipment record.
You do not have to upload every document immediately. Our team can review your request while you gather remaining files. However, uploading documents early helps speed up approval.

4. Wait for approval and track progress

After submitting your quote request, return to My Shipments (/portal) to monitor status. Your new request appears in the list — initially under Pending Quote Requests with status Pending Approval.

What happens during review

  1. Staff review — OMG Experience staff review your commodity selection, destination, pallet specifications, and uploaded documents. Status shows Awaiting staff review.
  2. Pricing — once approved for quoting, status moves to Awaiting Pricing. A note may appear: Staff will set rate soon or Destination & pricing will be set by our team — you can still upload documents anytime.
  3. Document review — if documents are missing, status may show Pending Documents. Upload remaining files from the shipment detail page.
  4. Confirmation — once approved and priced, the status progresses through booking, preparation, and shipping stages (see status reference below).

How to check your status

  1. Go to My Shipments from the sidebar (see Figure 6).
  2. Look for your request in the Pending Quote Requests section.
  3. Each card shows the quotation number, destination, Pending Approval badge, and last updated date.
  4. Click Open & upload documents or the shipment card to open its detail page (Figure 13).
  5. Use the search bar to find a specific quotation by number or destination.
You can upload or replace documents at any time from the shipment detail page — even while waiting for approval. This helps our team complete their review faster.

Cancelling a request

If you need to withdraw a pending request, open the shipment and use the cancel option. You will be asked to confirm: Cancel this quote request? This cannot be undone.

Status reference

Your shipment moves through these stages from submission to delivery:

Pending Approval → Awaiting Pricing → Pending Documents → Pending Booking → Booking Requested → Preparing → Waiting for Pick Up → Shipped → Delivered
StatusWhat it meansYour action
Pending Approval Your quote request has been submitted and is awaiting staff review. Wait for review. Upload any missing documents.
Awaiting Pricing Approved for quoting; team is preparing your rate. Wait for pricing. Destination may show as Destination TBD until set.
Pending Documents Required export documents are still needed. Open the shipment and upload missing files.
Pending Booking Quote confirmed; awaiting flight booking. No action needed — team is arranging transport.
Booking Requested Air freight space has been requested with the carrier. Monitor for updates.
AWB Received Air Waybill number received from the airline. Note the AWB for tracking.
Preparing Shipment is being prepared for collection or departure. Ensure cargo is ready for pick-up.
Waiting for Pick Up Cargo is ready and awaiting carrier collection. Be available for handover.
Shipped Cargo has departed on the booked flight. Track via shipment detail page.
Delivered Shipment completed successfully. Archive records as needed.

Dashboard summary cards group these statuses: Total, In Progress (active stages), Shipped, and Delivered. Delivered shipments are hidden by default — toggle to view them.

Troubleshooting

ProblemLikely causeSolution
Google sign-in blocked or rejected Browser automation, incorrect credentials, or account not registered Use a normal browser (not automated). Verify your Google email/password. Register first at /site/register.
This account is not registered as a customer Account exists but is a staff account Use the staff login at /login instead, or register a customer account.
Redirected back to login after signing in Session expired or cookies blocked Enable cookies for cargo.omgexp.com. Clear cache and sign in again.
File upload fails File too large or wrong format Ensure each file is under 10 MB and in PDF, JPG, PNG, or Excel format.
Storage upload failed Network interruption during upload Check your connection and retry the upload from the shipment page.
Shipment not visible on dashboard Not yet assigned to your account New accounts may see: Your shipments will appear here once our team assigns quotations to your account. Email cargo@omgexp.com if your submitted request does not appear within 24 hours.
Status stuck on Pending Approval Review in progress or missing documents Upload all required documents. Email cargo@omgexp.com with your quotation number if waiting more than 2 business days.
